Snow
International

Snow remote sensing has been underway for more than 40 years, and is today (in a warming world) more critical than ever. While there are a number of societies and unions that deal with snow in general, there has not been a society or group specifically focused on seasonal snow and remote sensing. Nonetheless, a real snow and remote sensing community exists and understands it has a clear mission: to provide the techniques and methods needed to answer the world's pressing snow problems. This website is the home for that community.
