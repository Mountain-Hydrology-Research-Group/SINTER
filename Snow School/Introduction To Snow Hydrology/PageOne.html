Getting Started

Hello and welcome to the course!

The instructors for this course are Dr. Nick Rutter of the University of Northumbria, and Dr. Alexandre Langlois of the University of Sherbrooke. This course will be in six parts, once a week. This course is brought to you by CUASHI and SINTER. To watch it live, you can register at CUASHI.org.

After each course, there will be an assignment for you to do. These assignments are not graded, but we encourage you to try them!

If this is the first SINTER course that you have taken, feel free to visit the introduction lesson for a quick run through of the interface. Happy Learning!
